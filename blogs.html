The natural scenic beauty and peaceful environment naturally appeal to the people, and its green natural views can surely soothe their inner senses.

BIOPHILIA IS THE WAY TO HEALING

“To experience biophilia is to love a diversity that, as limitless as it is fragile, both haunts us and fills us with hope. ” ― Adam Leith Gollner. The idea of biophilia is the notion that humans possess an instinctive tendency to seek out connections with nature and other forms of life. There’s a growing body of anecdotal and scientific research that validates the benefits generated by a proximity to nature—or elements that mimic it—and supports the trend toward nature-centric design. we have depended on nature for almost our entire existence during which nature has become critical to human’s physical-mental health, and well-being. Modern world forces – especially indoor environments devoid of nature – have a negative impact on the human mind in the long run. And in current times, where the world is rapidly urbanizing, it is extremely crucial to understand the mental health benefits of nature. Embedding nature and natural systems in urban design and indoor, built environments is important in order to keep the human mind, body, and sense connected to a bio-centric world. tshé valley’s design and layout have been developed keeping the biophilia and nature integrated lifestyle in mind and this is what makes it the best resort in Rajgarh, Himachal.

ADVENTURES AT RAJGARH

Away from the hue and cry of the busy life in big cities, most working people like to flee to a quiet place like the peach valley Rajgarh where they can relax, enjoy themselves with their family and come back to work, all rejuvenated. Rajgarh, is a modern paradise with all luxuries of life yet very traditional, peaceful, and away from pollution. Situated in the beautiful hills covered with tall trees, beautiful flowers, and a variety of flora and fauna, this town is the best place for people in Delhi, Chandigarh, and nearby areas to sneak out on the weekend. Luxury villas in Rajgarh offer great amenities and adventure activities for you and your family. tshé Valley in Rajgarh, a well-known five-star community complex, is one of the few that provide well-furnished standard, premium, luxury villas with modern facilities, fine dining restaurant, banquet facilities, safe parking area, doctor on call facility, Wi-fi, and internet facility, pool table,laundry facilities and a conference hall for your official meeting are all available under the roof of this Rajgarh Villa. Weather in Rajgarh is pleasant most times of the year, and though summer is the best time of the year to enjoy, the calm and pleasant weather in winters is enjoyable too, and the beauty adds on with rare snowing. For those fond of adventure, trekking and camping in Rajgarh are ideal activities in these woods. One can also choose rock climbing, rock rappelling, rope bridge, paragliding, and skiing when it snows for an adventurous Rajgarh experience.
