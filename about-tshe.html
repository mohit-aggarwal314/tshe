About Us

The tshé Valley Wellness Resorts are luxury lodges spread over eight acres that offer exquisite accommodations accompanied by an emphasis on healing and nourishing the soul. A cherishable life can only be achieved by integrating the human soul with nature. A visit to this region would leave one mesmerized by nature's beauty with its opulent designs and bright sunshine brisking through the trees to light the design's magnificence. Surrounded by lush verdure, at tshé Valley, we have an international 5-star hotel Brand (coming soon) and a world-class wellness center with efficacious medical therapies administered by renowned and highly trained therapists.

A TRIPHE’S Group Enterprise

The TRIPHE’S Group is a disparate collection of boutique businesses that operate in a wide range of industries today. These include food processing, domestic fuel distribution, real estate start-ups, hospitality consulting and development, and real estate start-ups (for second homes that focus on wellness). Over thirty years ago, the top hoteliers in India formed our flagship company, Triumph Hospitality & Psyche Hospitality Enterprises Ltd., a pioneer in the hospitality and tourism industries.

Over these decades, TRIPHE’S Group has established multifaceted hospitality projects, increasing its exposure to cutting-edge developments. It currently aims to develop a portfolio of lifestyle places for a select group of affluent individuals that will promise to have a noticeable valuable influence on all stakeholders.
